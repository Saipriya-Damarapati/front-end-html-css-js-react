2. Product Inventory Management
You are managing a product inventory. Create an object product with the following properties:
name: "Laptop"
price: 60000
stock: 50
Tasks:
1.	Write a method updateStock(newStock) to update the stock property.
2.	Add a method applyDiscount(percent) to reduce the price by the given percentage.
3.	Display the updated details of the product.
